Brand Guidelines

Brand Overview

Key Message

Budgeting should be easy...and even fun!

The Why

When people budget they take control of their finances. Budgeting is the core behavior associated with Dave's principles of financial success.

Audience should feel

Disarmed and at-ease. Budgeting (and anything financial) can feel intimidating. We want EveryDollar to feel approachable, fun, and energetic.

Brand guardrails

Simple, Happy, Functional
